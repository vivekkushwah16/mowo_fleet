Creating a generation of confident women in mobility

What is MOWO Fleet?

Having women change things from the inside is a way to transform broken systems. Our Fleet is MOWO - MOving WOmen and we are building a tech enabled Women-only Fleet. This fleet will provide a mix of deliveries and women only on-demand taxi services.
