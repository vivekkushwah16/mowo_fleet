What is MOWO Fleet?

Having women change things from the inside is a way to transform broken systems. Our Fleet is MOWO - MOving WOmen and we are building a tech enabled Women-only Fleet. This fleet will provide a mix of deliveries and women only on-demand taxi services.

SRISHTI Bakshi

Srishti Bakshi is a women’s rights activist who embarked on a 3800 kms on-foot journey across India from Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu to Srinagar, Kashmir determined to sow the seeds of gender equality and became a role model for several thousand girls and boys nationwide. Deeply shocked by a case reported from India, where a car with a family was stopped on the highway 91 and the mother and daughter were dragged away and gang-raped in front of the father, Srishti decided not be a silent spectator to rising crime against women in her country. She left a thriving career and her comfortable life in Hong Kong and started on the most unusual path. On her journey Srishti met over hundred thousand people, sensitizing them to the gender-based violence and inequality in the country. Her aim was to combat it by empowering women themselves with confidence, leadership skills, digital and financial literacy that would allow them to be financially independent through livelihoods. Her journey has now been shared across the world in the form of a documentary WOMB Women of my Billion due to release on Netflix at the end of the year. The President of India extended his support to her campaign and Srishti was named an Champion for Change 2016-2017 by The UN Women. Srishti was also conferred with the Commonwealth Points of Light award, the highest accolades from the British Commonwealth which she received from Her Majesty the Queen. Srishti has been recognized as an influential change-maker and thought-leader and has been a frequent speaker at conferences organized by the UN, US Embassy, Swedish Institute, National Commissions for Women; Knowledge Roundtables featuring top legal minds, activists and women leaders; she has participated and spoken at the Gender Equality Summit by the UN Global Compact Network; and been a part of #SHEROESHours, a multi-city event to #PressforProgress throughout the corporate world. Srishti was the closing plenary speaker at the IFC Conference in Holland organised by The Resource Alliance.
